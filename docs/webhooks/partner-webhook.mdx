## Setting up your webhook

You set up your webhook URL by signing up for an Every.org account and creating one in the [developer dashboard](https://www.every.org/developer).
[Staging environment webhooks](https://staging.every.org/developer) are also available to test, but requires an [explicit email verification](https://staging.every.org/verify-email) after account creation.
